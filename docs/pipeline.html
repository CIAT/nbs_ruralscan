Variable selection (sub-step in M1 & M3) — Ani's three principles

  • Reduce — thematic grouping (per recipe) + correlation cluster (per AOI). One representative per cluster enters MCDA.
  • Source — native GEE → community GEE → upload-to-GEE. Fitness-for-purpose precedes platform.
  • Explain — every variable shipped as a Variable Card: What · Why · How to read · Cluster · Source · Interpretation.
